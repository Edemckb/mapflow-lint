# Map Review Checklist

## Route

- The entrance is immediately readable.
- The critical path can be explained in one sentence.
- At least one optional branch has a meaningful reward.
- Every dead end is intentional.
- The player can return from optional content.

## Pacing

- The route provides an early understanding or small win.
- Challenge escalates instead of remaining flat.
- Combat nodes are separated by exploration, decisions, or recovery when needed.
- A safe threshold exists before the climax.

## Spatial logic

- Doors, bridges, terrain, and interiors connect logically.
- Landmarks are visible from useful decision points.
- Shortcuts reduce repeated traversal without removing all tension.

## Encounters and rewards

- Enemies pressure movement or protect something valuable.
- Rewards are not placed without effort, discovery, or context.
- Safe spaces do not contain accidental combat pressure.

## Playtest questions

- Where did the player hesitate?
- Which branch did they miss?
- Did they understand the objective without explanation?
- Was any encounter repeated without adding a new decision?
- Could they find the return path?
